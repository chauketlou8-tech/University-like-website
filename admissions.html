Application Process

Follow these steps to join our academic community

1

Complete Application Form

Fill out our online application with your personal information, academic history, and program preferences.

  • Online application portal
  • No application fee
  • Save and return anytime
2

Submit Academic Records

Provide official transcripts from all previously attended institutions.

  • High school transcripts
  • College transcripts (if applicable)
  • Official English translations
3

Letters of Recommendation

Submit 2-3 letters from teachers, professors, or professionals who know you well.

  • Academic references preferred
  • Professional references accepted
  • Digital submission available
4

Personal Statement

Share your story, goals, and why you're interested in our university.

  • 500-800 words
  • Discuss your aspirations
  • Highlight your achievements
5

Additional Requirements

Some programs may require additional materials like portfolios, auditions, or test scores.

  • Portfolio (Art/Design programs)
  • Audition (Performing Arts)
  • Standardized test scores (optional)
6

Submit & Track Application

Review your application and submit. Track your status through our applicant portal.

  • Application status updates
  • Admissions decision timeline
  • Financial aid information

Admission Requirements

Undergraduate

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA (on 4.0 scale)
  • Personal statement
  • 2 letters of recommendation
  • Official transcripts

Graduate

  • Bachelor's degree from accredited institution
  • Minimum 3.0 GPA
  • GRE/GMAT scores (program dependent)
  • 3 letters of recommendation
  • Statement of purpose

International Students

  • TOEFL 80+ or IELTS 6.5+
  • Financial documentation
  • Passport copy
  • Visa eligibility
  • Credential evaluation
