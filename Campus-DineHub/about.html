Our Story

From a student project to campus-wide solution

2021

The Beginning

Founded by three computer science students who struggled to find affordable, healthy meals between classes. What started as a class project quickly became a passion.

2022

Campus Pilot

Launched our first pilot program serving 200 students in the Computer Science building. Received overwhelming positive feedback and expanded to three more locations.

2023

Full Campus Rollout

Partnered with campus dining services to expand across all major campus buildings. Introduced our mobile app and reached 10,000+ students.

2024

Future Vision

Planning expansion to three additional campuses and introducing sustainable packaging and more diverse menu options to cater to all dietary needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I become a campus delivery partner?

We're always looking for enthusiastic students to join our delivery team! You can apply through our careers page or visit our office in the Student Center. Requirements include being a current student with valid ID and having a reliable mode of transportation.

Do you accommodate special dietary requirements?

Absolutely! We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and halal options. You can specify your dietary requirements when placing your order, and our kitchen team will ensure your meal meets your needs. For severe allergies, please contact us directly so we can take extra precautions.

How can I suggest new menu items?

We love getting menu suggestions from our students! You can submit your ideas through our feedback form on the website or mobile app. We regularly review suggestions and test the most popular ones. If your suggestion gets implemented, we'll even give you free meals!

Do you offer catering for campus events?

Yes! We provide catering services for student organization events, department functions, and campus activities. We offer special group pricing and customizable menus. Please contact our partnerships team at least 72 hours before your event to discuss your needs.
