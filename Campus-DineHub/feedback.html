Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take for my review to appear?

Reviews are typically posted within 24 hours after moderation. We review all feedback to ensure it meets our community guidelines.

Can I edit my review after submitting?

Yes, you can edit your review within 7 days of submission. After that, you'll need to contact our support team to make changes.

Do you respond to all reviews?

We read every review and respond to feedback that requires specific follow-up or addresses important issues. We're committed to continuous improvement.

What happens to negative feedback?

We value all feedback, including negative reviews. They help us identify areas for improvement and make meaningful changes to enhance your experience.
