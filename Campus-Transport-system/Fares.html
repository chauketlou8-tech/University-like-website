Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get student discounts?

Present your valid University student card when boarding. For online bookings, your student status is automatically verified through the university system.

Can I share my transport pass?

No, transport passes are non-transferable and are linked to your student/staff ID for security reasons.

What happens if I lose my card?

Immediately report lost cards at the Transportation Office. Replacement cards cost R50 and existing balance can be transferred.

Are passes refundable?

Passes are non-refundable but can be transferred to the next semester under special circumstances with approval.

Do fares change during holidays?

Regular fares apply during term time. Reduced schedules may operate during holidays with the same fare strUniversityure.

How do I purchase a semester pass?

Semester passes can be purchased online through the student portal or at the Transportation Office during registration periods.
