Types of Financial Aid

We offer various financial assistance programs to support your educational journey

Scholarships

Merit-based awards that don't require repayment. Available for academic excellence, sports, and special talents.

  • Academic Excellence Awards
  • Sports Scholarships
  • Leadership Awards
  • Special Talent Grants

Bursaries

Financial assistance based on financial need. These awards help cover tuition and living expenses.

  • Need-Based Awards
  • Full & Partial Bursaries
  • Renewable Annually
  • Living Expense Support

Student Loans

Low-interest loan programs with flexible repayment options and grace periods after graduation.

  • Low Interest Rates
  • Flexible Repayment
  • Grace Periods
  • Co-signer Options

Work-Study Programs

On-campus employment opportunities that allow you to earn while you learn valuable skills.

  • On-Campus Jobs
  • Flexible Hours
  • Career Experience
  • Skill Development
